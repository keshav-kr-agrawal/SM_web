Cancellation & Refunds

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

At Sparsh Mukthi, we strive to provide the best touchless control experience. This policy outlines our cancellation and refund procedures for subscription services.

1. Free Trial Period

  • New users receive a 4-month free trial (2 months + 2 bonus months) with full access to all features.
  • You can cancel anytime during the trial period without any charges.
  • No credit card is required to start the free trial.
  • If you cancel during the trial, you retain access until the trial period ends.

2. Subscription Cancellation

  • You can cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings or by contacting support at support@sparshmukthi.com.
  • Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing cycle.
  • You will continue to have access to Sparsh Mukthi until the end of the paid period.
  • After cancellation, your subscription will not auto-renew, and you will not be charged further.
Important: Canceling your subscription does not automatically qualify you for a refund. Please review our refund policy below.

3. Refund Policy

We offer refunds under the following conditions:

3.1 7-Day Money-Back Guarantee

  • If you're not satisfied with Sparsh Mukthi, you can request a full refund within 7 days of your first paid subscription charge.
  • This applies only to your first paid subscription (after the free trial).
  • To request a refund, contact us at support@sparshmukthi.com with your order details.

3.2 Technical Issues

  • If you experience technical problems that prevent you from using Sparsh Mukthi and our support team is unable to resolve the issue within 14 days, you may be eligible for a prorated refund.
  • You must report technical issues promptly and allow our team reasonable time to address them.

3.3 Billing Errors

  • If you were charged incorrectly or multiple times due to a billing error, we will refund the erroneous charges immediately.
  • Please contact us within 30 days of the charge with proof of the billing error.

4. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will not be provided in the following cases:

  • Cancellations made after the 7-day money-back guarantee period.
  • Partial month refunds (if you cancel mid-cycle, you keep access until the end of the billing period).
  • Renewal charges if you did not cancel before the renewal date.
  • If you violate our Terms and Conditions, resulting in account termination.
  • Change of mind after actively using the service beyond the refund period.
  • Incompatibility with your device if it does not meet our published system requirements.

5. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, follow these steps:

  • Step 1: Email us at support@sparshmukthi.com with the subject line "Refund Request".
  • Step 2: Include your account email, order/transaction ID, and reason for the refund request.
  • Step 3: Our support team will review your request within 3-5 business days.
  • Step 4: If approved, refunds will be processed to your original payment method within 7-10 business days.

6. Refund Processing Time

  • Approved refunds are processed within 7-10 business days.
  • The time it takes for the refund to appear in your account depends on your bank or payment provider (typically 5-10 business days).
  • You will receive an email confirmation once the refund has been initiated.

7. Promotional Offers & Discounts

  • If you subscribed using a promotional code or discount, the refund amount will reflect the actual amount paid, not the original price.
  • Promotional codes and bonuses (e.g., reward points, coupons) are non-refundable once redeemed.

8. Auto-Renewal & Prevention

  • Subscriptions automatically renew at the end of each billing cycle unless canceled.
  • You will receive an email reminder 7 days before your subscription renews.
  • To avoid unwanted charges, cancel your subscription before the renewal date.
  • We are not responsible for charges incurred if you fail to cancel before auto-renewal.

9. Account Deletion

  • If you delete your account, any active subscription will be canceled immediately.
  • Deleting your account does not automatically trigger a refund. Please request a refund separately if eligible.
  • Once deleted, your account data cannot be recovered.

10. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Cancellation & Refunds Policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Effective Date." Continued use of Sparsh Mukthi after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
