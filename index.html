About Sparsh Mukthi

Empowering touchless interaction for everyone

Our Mission

To make technology accessible to all by enabling touchless interaction through gestures, voice, and head tracking.

Our Vision

Building an inclusive future where everyone, regardless of ability, can interact with digital devices effortlessly.

The Story

Sparsh Mukthi was born from the desire to break barriers in technology access. Inspired by real-world challenges faced by people with disabilities, our team set out to create a solution that leverages modern AI and sensor technology for universal usability.

UN SDG Alignment & Social Impact

Aligned with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), Sparsh Mukthi advances Goal 3: Good Health and Well-being, Goal 4: Quality Education, and Goal 10: Reduced Inequalities by making digital access equitable and empowering.

Domains Covered

  • Education: Enabling students with disabilities to learn and participate fully.
  • Healthcare: Assisting patients and caregivers with hands-free device control.
  • VR Gaming: Making immersive experiences accessible to all.
